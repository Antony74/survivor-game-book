You remain a gladiator, albeit as a free man. Many more unfortunates die under the blows of your sword. You continue to captivate audiences. Your fame extends beyond the borders of Baszar and masters of arms come from other cities to confront you, but none of them can overcome your skill, finesse and craftsmanship. 
As a liberator, you can fully enjoy the fruits of your victories. Between battles you can drink expensive wines, eat delicacies and have fun with women. However, you begin to wonder if all this entertainment will make you too... Soft. Slow. Weak. 
You live once! I won't be giving up the pleasure. I will simply be choosing weaker opponents (page 53). 
Okay, that's it, you need to get upright. I'm a fighter, not a rake (page 16).
